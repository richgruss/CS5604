Day eight of the Queensland election campaign sees premier Campbell Newman struggling for a synonym, as he utters the word 'strong' more than 20 times during a short press conference. He knows what he wants the Queensland people to think of when they think of him, and he's prepared to inject the word into every sentence to ensure it makes the television and radio grabs. The Guardian is only too happy to oblige
