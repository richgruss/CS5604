				About DFL CD2

The Democratic - Farmer - Labor (DFL) Party of Minnesota's Second Congressional District. CD2 covers the south Twin Cities metro area and contains all of Scott, Dakota, Goodhue, and Wabasha counties. It also contains part of northern and eastern Rice County including the city of Northfield, and southern Washington County including the city of Cottage Grove.
