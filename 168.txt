It has a light side, a dark side, an’ it holds t’Tennessee together.” Ramsey: Medicaid expansion ‘sellabull’ n' TennesseeThar Tale be told on December 12, 2014 | By The Voluntaer State Mountin Man Electyuns(Photo: File photo)Nashvull, Tenn. – Senate Speek'r Ron Ramsey sez at if'n fella Republicun Gov. Bill Haslam succeeds n' his'n negotiatyuns wit Presdint Barock Obamie’s administrashun un a speshul Tennessee deel fer Medicaid expansion, t'result could be “sellabull” ta skeptical state lawmakers.It’s a change n' tone fer t'Blountville Republicun, who as resantlee as Novemb'r o'las year sed Haslam wuz “wastin his'n time,” an' at he would be agin un effurt ta expan' Medicaid eve if'n t'governer could git his'n deel.T' biggest problem, as Ramsey sees it, is at he doesn’t reckon t'Obamie administrashun is innerested n' agreeyun' ta Haslam’s proposal ta have copays an' deductibles an' use t'fedral money ta subsidize commercial insurance.“If'n we a'ken git ta at, at may be sumthin we a'ken sell,” Ramsey tole reperters Thirsdee.Ramsey sed copays, deductibull an' personal heelth coachyun' air elemants o'his'n own heelth coverage n' t'state plun, which he sed helps encourage personal responsibilty.“If'n y'all could take at down ta at populashun an' still cov'r 'um, I reckon at would be sellabull,” he sed. “I’m jes reluctant ta reckon at t'current administrashun n' Washington D.C. would alloe innythang like at.”Haslam has sed he will eethur strike un agreement by t'end o't' year er aban'on his'n efferts fer whut he calls his'n Tennessee Plun altogeth'r.Heelth keer advocates have heevilee criticizet Haslam fer refusyun' las year ta agree ta $1.4 billyun n' fedral funds ta cov'r about 180,000 uninsuret Tennesseens und'r t'erms t'money wuz offerd. Nine uther Republicun governers have agreet ta expan'et Medicaid, an' at leest three mer air n' discussyuns wit fedral officials.A Van'erbilt poll releeset las week sed 56 persent suppertid expan'yun' Medicaid fer loe-income Tennesseens. But inny deel would lackly face a tough path n' bof chambers o't' Genrull Assemblee, whar Republikuns hold vas majeritees.State lawmakers earli'r thishere year enactid legislayshun fer t'governer ta obtane thar apperval befor agreeyun' ta Medicaid expansion n' whut supperters dubbet t'“Stop Obamacare Ack.”T' governer has bee guardet about inny specifics o't' proposal amid talks wit t'fedral gummint. Ramsey sed he expects t'governer ta releese t'full deetails o't' proposal eve if'n it is ultimatelee rejectid un t'fedral level.“He’s bee reluctant ta put innythang un t'abull, becawz everthang is n' negotiashun,” Ramsey sed. “If'n thay git clos'r ta a final product an' thay turn it down, the I reckon y'all’ll see at final product an' see at thishere is whut thay turnt down an' at it looks reesonabull.”Copyriite 2014 T' Associatid Press.
