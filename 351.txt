Social Media Team Call- Using Social Media to Intervene in Key LA, NC Senate Races
The National Draft Ben Carson for President Committee invites you to help turn the tide in key senate races for Republican candidates.  We will discuss ways in which we might use Social Media to get the positive message out about Republican candidates and spread the gospel truth about the Democrats and their philosophy of divide and conquer.
We urge you to be on the lookout for an email from Tonya Mead, Social Media Director this weekend.  In the email, she will provide Social Media Tip Sheet #3 and other goodies to help with this monumental effort. Please join us and earn the bragging rights to say, "Ya, I participated and helped the US Senators from Louisiana and North Carolina win 10 to 20% of the minority vote!"
On Monday Sept 15, at 8pm Eastern, 7pm Central, 6pm Mountain, 5pm Pacific
Dial 1-857-232-0156 and give code 270490#
