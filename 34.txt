Election Watch 2014!!: Needles, CA: Oath of Office administrated to the newly elected.
The Oath of Office was administrated to the newly elected City of Needles Mayor and Needles City Council during the Needles City Council Meeting held on Tuesday, December 9th, 2014 in Needles, California.
Before the administration the Oath of Office, members of the Needles City Council unanimously approved Item #5 which was to approve Resolution No. 2014-76 Reciting the Fact of the General Municipal Election held on Tuesday, November 4th, 2014, declaring the results, and such other matters as provided by law.
http://www.cityofneedles.com/2014%20website%20file%20posting/1%20-%20City%20Council%20-%20NPUA%20-%20SARDA/12%20-%20December%202014/cc_120914-5.pdf
San Bernardino County 1st District Supervisor Robert A. Lovingood administrated the Oath of Office to re-elected City of Needles Mayor Edward T. Paget, new Needles City Council Member Louise Evans, new Needles City Council Robert Richardson, M.D. and new Needles City Council Jeff Williams who all won the community’s vote during the 2014 Statewide General Elections held on Tuesday, November 4th, 2014.

After administrating the Oath of Office and congratulating the new members of the Needles City Council, re-elected City of Needles Mayor Edward T. Paget gave thanks and presented to outgoing Vice Mayor and Needles City Council Member Terry Campbell, Needles City Council Member Shawn Gudmundson, and Needles City Council Member Linda Kidd with each a plaque commemorating their years of service. 
Outgoing Vice Mayor and Needles City Council Member Terry Campbell thanked the community for letting him serve for 4 years on the Needles City Council.
Outgoing Needles City Council Member Shawn Gudmundson said it was a pleasure to serve the community and told the crowd that he is thinking of running again in 2 years. 
Outgoing Needles City Council Member Linda Kidd thanked everyone for allowing her to serve the community and wished the new members of the Needles City Council good luck and hopes that they’ll continue a lot of the good things the outgoing members of the Needles City Council has in the pipeline.
San Bernardino County 1st District Supervisor Robert A. Lovingood presented plaques to outgoing Vice Mayor and Needles City Council Member Terry Campbell, Needles City Council Member Shawn Gudmundson, and Needles City Council Member Linda Kidd for their service to the community, and told the crowd that he’d enjoyed that good working relationship with the outgoing members of the Needles City Council and hopes that the relationship continues with the new members of the Needles City Council and saying to the new members of the Needles City Council to not hesitate to call him and that his office is committed to Needles, California in working proactively on issues that may arise.
Eldon Heaston, Executive Director of the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District, presented outgoing Vice Mayor and Needles City Council Member Terry Campbell a plaque for his service with the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District and hopes to work with the new members of the Needles City Council.
As the Needles City Council Meeting continued, new Needles City Council Jeff Williams was voted by members of the Needles City Council to be the Vice Mayor on the Needles City Council.
The Needles City Council Meeting for Tuesday, December 23rd, 2014 is canceled for Christmas and New Year’s so the new members of the Needles City Council will be meeting to work on the community in January 2015.
