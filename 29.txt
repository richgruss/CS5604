Documentary Film Overview

             from The Script & Film Co. PRO 4 months ago

All Audiences






This is a ten minute video especially produced for an Indiegogo crowd funding campaign. It is hosted and narrated by the documentary's filmmaker Peter Smoczynski. The video outlines an investigative documentary "Election Day In Canada: When Voter Suppression Comes Calling". This outlook introduces the subject matter (2011 Federal Election, Robocalls, voter suppression, Bill C-23 or the Fair Elections Act and Elections Canada investigation and termination of the Robocalls investigation) and what is needed for completion. The documentary proper will be narrated by veteran Canadian actor Jennifer Dale (Diamond Road)
