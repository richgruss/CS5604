Delhi Election 2015 Results Live Update on Counting

By vijay
 Feb 09th,2015





































 Delhi election result, Delhi election result 2015, Delhi election update, Delhi election news, Delhi election 2015 counting, Delhi election 2015 counting status, Delhi new chief minister: The counting for Delhi Election Result 2015 has begun. Delhi voted to elect their New Chief Minister on February 7 and the fight is beatween Arvind Kejriwal led Aam Admi Party (AAP) and Kiran Bedi's Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). We bring you the counting status and live election news update on Pallibatani.com
Arvind Kejriwal to meet Prime Minister Narendra Modi today
Celebrations began outside AAP office in Patel Nagar
Delhi Polls: AAP leading in 50 seats
AAP's Naresh Balyan is leading in Uttam Nagar
East Delhi: AAP -6, BJP -2 (leading)
Alka Lamba of AAP leads in Congress bastion Chandni Chowk

Rajesh Rishi of AAP leads in Janakpuri

Ajay Maken trailing in Sadar Bazar
Results not a referendum on Narendra Modi, says Kiran Bedi

Congress leads in Chadni Chowk and Karol Bagh

Kiran Bedi leads from Krishna Nagar in first round counting
