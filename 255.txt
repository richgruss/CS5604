16-year-olds should be bull ta votePublished November 6, 2014 | By T' Neck Hizzelf 
	Un Eeleckshun Day, mer thun 8 million Amurkins who air purfeckly capabull o'makin infermet an' reesonet politickull decisyuns wuz deniet t'privilege o'votin. I’m a'talkin about 16- an' 17-year-olds. 
	Ov'r t'las 40 yeers, thar has bee occasyunal discusshun n' t'Unitid States about loweryun' t'votin age, un idee at has bee gunyun' popularitee roun t'worl. N' parts o'Yurop an' much o'South Amurka t'votin age is 16 er 17. T' Unitid Kingdom is debatyun' a'lettin 16-year-olds ta vote, an' t'discusshun has pickt up steem a'followin Scotlan'’s resent referendum un independence, n' which thems 16 an' olt'r wuz permittid ta vote – an' did so n' greet numbers. Studies o'electyuns n' countries at permit 16-year-olds ta vote un state an' local matters find high turnout n' thishere age group as well.
	Societees have long grapplet wit whar ta draw t'legal boundree tween adolessence an' adulthood; today neerly all countries use 18 as t'age o'majeritee. T' Unitid States stan's apart frum mos o't' worl n' at it uses differnt ages fer differnt rights an' responsibilitees. We permit folk ta drif' wen thay air 16 (eve young'r n' a few states), but prohibit 'um frum purchasyun' alkyhol until thay air 21. T' ages at which adolessants a'ken see a sexy moovee pitshure shue, marry, ent'r into contracts, er buy cigarettes fall tween these acoupla extremes.

Each apperach – usin a sangle age fer all legal boundree-drawyun' er decidyun' each issue un a case by case basis – has merits an' drawbax. T' one-age-fits-all regime has t'advantages o'consistency, claritee an' fairness. Oncet y'all’re un adult, y'all’re un adult. If'n y'all’re old enough ta wurk, pay taxes an' serve n' t'militree, y'all ort ta be old enough ta drank, smoke, drif' an' vote.
	T' issue-specific apperach is potantiallee wis'r becawz it permits socidey ta align legal responsibilitees an' privileges wit folk’s abilitees an' needs. Howev'r, n' practiss we creete legal boundaries fer a cumplex mix o'politickull an' practical reesons, which frekwantlee lead ta laws at make lil sense. Surelee no one thinks it’s easi'r ta han'le a car thun a be'r. But sum 16-year-olds neet ta drif' becawz public transpertashun is limitid n' mos parts o't' kuntry, an' t'drankin age is 21, n' part becawz we wurry about yung folk drankin an' drivin. (Ackshly, countries wit a har drivin age but a low'r drankin age have fur saf'r highways thun we do.) If'n we uset t'science o'adolessent development ta guide air a'thankin about t'age o'majeritee, we mite reech differnt conclusyuns about wen ta extend adult privileges ta yung folk. T' votin age is a case n' point.
	Science duz nairy point ta un obveeus chronological age at which a brite-line legal boundree tween adolessants an' adults should be drawn fer all purposes, but it is instructif'. Mos aspects o'emotyunal an' intellectual maturitee reech adult levels sumtime tween 15 an' 22. Adolessants’ judgment n' situatyuns at permit meesuret deecishun-makin an' consultashun wit utherns – whut psychologists call “cold cognishun” – is jes as mature as at o'adults by 16. N' contras, adolessants’ judgment n' situatyuns at evoke “hot cognishun” – situatyuns n' which thar emotyuns air arouset, time preshure is a facter an' thay air n' groups – is nairy fullee mature until thay air olt'r, certunlee no earli'r thun 18 an' maybe as late as 21.

	If'n science wuz un impertant considerashun n' a'settin t'age o'majeritee – as I reckon it should be – a reesonabull startin point would be ta distinguish tween acoupla sets o'laws: thems fer activitees at involve cold cognishun an' thems fer ones involvyun' hot cognishun. Cold cognishun is relevant ta matters such as votin er grantyun' infermet consent fer medicull procedures, fer eggzample. Adolessants a'ken gath'r evidence, consult wit utherns an' take time befor makin a deecishun. Adolessants may make bad choices, but statisticallee speakin, thay won’t make 'um inny mer ofte thun adults.
	A lat'r age o'majeritee is mer sensibull fer matters at involve hot cognishun, such as drivin, drankin an' criminal responsibilty. Here t'circumstances air usual thems at brang out t'wurse n' adolessants’ judgment – thay frekwantlee pit t'emptayshun o'immediate rewards agin t'prudent considerashun o'long-term costs, an' occur wen folk air emotyunallee arouset, an' air influencet by thar peers. Fer these serts o'matters, t'age o'legal adulthood ort ta be 18.
