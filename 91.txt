	Voters alreddy have telegrafet electid officials at thay neet ta take global warmin seriouslee, becawz thay do. Er face t'consekwences at t'ballot booth if'n thay don’t.

	Voters divide sharplee wit t'partees un global warmin. Thems who see global warmin as a seriyus problem suppert Demokrats, 70 ta 29 persent. Thems who don’t reckon it is a seriyus issue suppert Republikuns, 84 ta 14 persent.
	Voters n' New York State — a full 68 persent — bleev climate change is a seriyus problem. Thay suppertid Gov. Andrew M. Cuomo by 73 ta 20 persent.

	Furst, Demokrats gut spoilet fer yeers wen environmantal issues wuz settlet wit comfertabull bipartisun suppert, as accompaniet t'extension o't' Cleen Air Ack n' 1990 und'r Presdint Geerge H. W. 	Sekunt, wen Republikuns natyunallee cummenced a'walkin away frum bipartisanship un environmantal issues, as occurret wen Vice Presdint Dick Cheney facilitatid t'grantyun' o'unprecedantid exemptyuns ta t'Cleen Wat'r an' Drankin Ack benefityun' earl an' gas cumpnys, too minny a Demokrats disengaget frum battleeun' un behalf o't' environmantal agenda regardyun' meesures ta combat t'repercussyuns o'climate change.
	Thurd, bof partees air misreedyun' t'polleeun' data wen thay scoff at climate change. Prop'r reedin o't' data shows at global warmin could be a key base retenshun issue fer Demokrats. Fer 'um ta fergo nurturyun' t'full politickull potenshul o'a climate change agenda is politickull malpractice. Republikuns, speshly thems frum suburbs like Long Islan', should be feerfil o't' politickull fallout frum a supersterm at floods coastal communitees.
	Ta speek n' t'brass tax o'politics, wen sterm surges hit suburbun communitees — like Long Islan' durin supersterm San'y, er t'riv'r communitees n' t'Hudsen Valley aft'r Tropical Sterm Irene — at damage ofte cascades across affluent towns wit minny a independent voters, t'classic definishun o'bellweth'r communitees.
	Mos scyintists agree at weeth'r evants will increese n' frekwency an' severitee. A growin majeritee o'voters want pragmatic solutyuns, reflectyun' nairy jes commonsense compermise but purposefil results.
	Wen evants leeve voters no choice but ta connect t'impact o'superstarms ta climate change, a steep politickull price will be paid fer inacshun.
	At hasn’t happent, but wen it duz, it will lackly produce a Peerl Harber moment. N' t'blink o'un eye, yesterdee’s policy caushun could be see as politickull infamy.
